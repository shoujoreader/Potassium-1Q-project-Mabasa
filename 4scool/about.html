Reason why...

I chose this topic for my website because of my excessive feelings towards the stigma and judgement that mentally unstable people face, as I have also been judged and stereotyped in the past. I remember how hurt I was by other peoples opinions on top of struggling to keep myself in check because of my unstable mental health, the numbness of my body and the way I felt unbearable to be with other people all the while I loathed myself because of the self stigma. Then I found out that a lot of other people also went through what I did and ciuld help but feel bad. I don't ever want others to go through anything like that or worse, so I felt like making this the topic to write my thoughts and spread awareness about the issue. Showing people how unfair judgement just because of a persons condition is the best thing I can do at the moment.
